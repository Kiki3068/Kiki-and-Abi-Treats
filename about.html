About Us

Welcome to Kiki & Abi’s Treats, your home for authentic Nigerian flavors and handcrafted delicacies.

Our journey began with a simple love for traditional spices and the joy of sharing hearty, home-cooked meals with family and friends. Today, we bring that same passion to every pastry and dish we prepare.

From our signature "Moist Meat Pies" to our "Jolly Jollof Rice," every treat is made with premium ingredients and a touch of home.
