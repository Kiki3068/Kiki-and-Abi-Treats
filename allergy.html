Allergy Information

Please note: While some allergens are not used as primary ingredients, they may be present in trace amounts due to our suppliers or shared production environments.

Found in our egg rolls and the golden shine for all our pastries.

Fish

Found in our fish rolls and in our hake/titus stews.

Crustaceans (Crayfish)

Found in our Native meals.

Molluscs

Found in some of our stews.

Gluten / Wheat

Found in all pastries and in pasta.

Peanuts

Found in our Suya Wings and all meals with suya spice.

Milk

Found in some of our pastries.

Soya Beans
